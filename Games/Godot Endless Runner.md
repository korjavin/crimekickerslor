# Godot Endless Runner

A 3D Godot prototype — *"Zelda-like"* exploration on an endless, procedurally
generated field. The home of the most detailed **character designs and 3D models**.

## What it is

- Endless procedurally generated terrain; third-person.
- **Character switching** (press E) cycles through the playable heroes.
- Collect golden coins.

## Lore it contributes

- **Detailed visual designs** for all four heroes — the source for most of the
  appearance detail on [[Windman]], [[Pho-boman]], [[Primm]], and [[Tiebi]] (the
  character docs live in `docs/characters/`).
- **High-detail 3D model** of Windman (multi-part mesh, ~4,800 vertices).

## Notes

- Naming here: *Phoboman*, *Tiebe/teibi* (scene files vary). See [[Naming Variants]].
- No explicit plot — design- and mechanics-driven; lore is implicit in the character
  docs.
