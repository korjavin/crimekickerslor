# Stealth Kickers (Tactical Stealth)

A real-time tactical stealth game (working team name **"Stealth Action Squad"**)
inspired by the *Commandos* series. HTML/JS prototype.

## What it is

Control a team of four heroes, combine their skills to **infiltrate an enemy base,
complete objectives, and escape undetected**. Mission objectives seen in-game:
*collect enemy intelligence → retrieve a security keycard → escape to the extraction
point.* Guards have vision cones and alert states (UNAWARE → SUSPICIOUS → ALERTED);
stealth uses light/shadow, crouching, and per-character modifiers.

## Roster in this build

> Like *Shadow Kickers*, this is an **early build that predates [[Pho-boman]]'s
> inclusion** in the team. The canon heroes featured here are:

- **Windman:** Gale Blast + **Disguise** (looks normal → harder to detect).
- **Teibi:** Shrink ("smaller than a cockroach," the "ultimate infiltrator") + Grow
  ("size of an elephant," crush enemies). → [[Tiebi]]
- **Primm:** Gravity Phase (through marked walls) + Katana Strike (dash takedown).

## Lore it contributes

- The clearest **mission framing** so far: an organized, militarized adversary with
  an "enemy base" — useful for fleshing out [[The Bad Guys]].
- A **transformation origin** for [[Tiebi|Teibi]]: *"once a normal human, transformed
  into a being with complete control over his size"* — predates and **conflicts**
  with the cyborg-fragment canon (see [[Open Questions & Contradictions]]).
- Reinforces [[Primm]]'s *Zer0*-like design and twin blades (here called *ninjatos*).
