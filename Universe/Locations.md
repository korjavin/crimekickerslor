# Locations

Places that appear across the source material. None are in the approved registry yet,
so all are **source material** pending canonization.

## School / cafeteria

The recurring home base in the [[Crime Kickers Hub]] continuity. *"One cafeteria"* is
in the team tagline; the cafeteria even drives [[Tiebi]]'s mood (tater tots). Related
school props: chairs ([[Primm]]), the radiator ([[C-036 — Wind Chill Factor]]), a
school bus ([[C-039 — Tiebe vs. The School Bus]]), detention ([[Mr. Pierce]]).

> The school setting is central to the Hub framing but sits uneasily with the canon
> cyborg origin — see [[Open Questions & Contradictions]].

## The mall (food court)

Setting of [[C-041 — The Mall Was a Mistake]].

## The Detention Dimension

An alternate dimension reached through a **portal** that [[Mr. Pierce]] discovers in
[[C-038 — Detention Dimension]]. The universe's clearest sci-fi/supernatural hook.

## Sky Isles

Referenced in the Hub's prompt examples (e.g. *"Windman + Pho-boman at Sky Isles"*).
A floating-islands locale — otherwise undeveloped.

## The enemy base

The infiltration target in [[Stealth Kickers (Tactical Stealth)]] — implies an
organized, militarized adversary (see [[The Bad Guys]]).

## The endless field

The infinite procedurally generated terrain of the [[Godot Endless Runner]], roamed
by [[Piglet Crocodile]]s. Likely an abstract gameplay space rather than a real place.
