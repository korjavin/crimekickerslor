# Lunch Lady

A recurring legend. Cafeteria staff at **GastroDefense Inc.**, food-safety
division, wrong floor. She is the indirect cause of [[Pho-boman]]'s existence
— her spilled cauldron of pho is the incident that fills the prototype's
helmet and triggers the AEGIS-4 fracture. She is never an antagonist; she
is simply present, unbothered, and correct.

> **Role archetype:** One word per season, always the right one.

## Canon snapshot (ratified 2026-06-14)

- **Recurring status:** confirmed.
- **Narrative function:** recurring legend — minimal presence, maximum weight.
  She says one word per season; it is always the right word.
- **S01E01 word:** *"Settle."* (Panel 4, top-right window, delivered with a
  ladle hovering over Pho-boman's helmet in the cauldron.)
- **Cause of Pho-boman:** her cart wheel snags a cable in Panel 2; her
  cauldron tips; the broth arc is the incident. She does not react.

## Appearance (detailed, ratified 2026-06-14)

> deeply calm deadpan face, steel-grey hair pulled back in a tight bun

### The steaming pho cauldron (ratified 2026-06-14)

> a large dented stainless-steel cauldron/stockpot of pho on top of the cart,
> heavy lid tipped off, reddish-orange broth, rising steam, a long-handled

*The broth that fills [[Pho-boman]]'s helmet. Reddish-orange — the one warm pop
of color against the beige lab.*
