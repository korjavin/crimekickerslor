# Primm

One of the four [[The Crime Kickers|Crime Kickers]]. The team's **gravity/space
manipulator** and close-combat specialist. Per [[Origin & Concept]], one of the two
fragments of the original fractured cyborg (Project AEGIS-4).

> *"Negotiates with gravity."* — tagline (Hub)

## Canon snapshot (registry)

- **Visual archetype:** stylistically and in silhouette **identical to *Zer0* from
  *Borderlands*** — a futuristic, stealthy, masked look. *(Zer0 is a third-party
  reference for design direction only.)*
- **Weapon:** close-quarters combat with **paired blades — two katanas**.
- **Powers:**
  - **Gravity-field manipulation.**
  - **Short-range teleportation** (blink).
  - **Phasing** — passes freely through solid objects and structures.

## Appearance (detailed)

From `godot-test1/docs/characters/primm.md`:

- **Build:** slim, lean, ~175–180 cm; flexible, built for blinking and gravity poses.
- **Head/face:** slightly elongated face, sharp focused eyes, short-to-medium dark
  brown hair; **high-tech goggles** with faintly blue-tinted transparent lenses.
- **Jacket:** sleek lab-coat-style jacket, **dark purple with silver trim**, open
  at the front.
- **Under-shirt:** black, with faint glowing blue lines in a subtle geometric pattern.
- **Trousers/boots:** dark blue fitted trousers tucked into medium black boots with
  silver accents.
- **Accessories:** small metallic orbs / mini-tools that **orbit the chest and waist**,
  glowing faint blue; black gloves with silver fingertips.
- **Power VFX:** gravity use ripples space and bends light slightly; teleport leaves
  **faint glowing blue trails**.

> In the game prototypes the suit is rendered as a fully masked purple high-tech
> ninja with a **cyan glowing visor**; weapons are described both as **katanas** and
> as **ninjatos** (twin blades on the back).

## Powers

- **Gravity manipulation** — Hub frames it as *mass nullification*: objects within
  arm's reach lose ~80% of their weight.
- **Teleportation** — short-range blink.
- **Phasing** — pass through specially "marked" walls/floors; becomes semi-transparent
  while phasing.
- **Katana strike / dash** — a swift forward dash that cuts down enemies in its path
  (a "swift, silent takedown").
