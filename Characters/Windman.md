# Windman

One of the four [[The Crime Kickers|Crime Kickers]]. Carries the team's control of
**air and wind**. Per [[Origin & Concept]], he is one of the four fragments of the
original fractured cyborg.

> *"Moves air. Moves opinions."* — tagline (Hub)

## Canon snapshot (registry)

- **Build:** humanoid structure.
- **Height:** exactly **180 cm**.
- **Hair:** chestnut / brown.
- **Signature facial feature:** a **blue-and-red eye bandage** (blindfold).
- **Equipment:** a **multicolored hand fan**.
- **Powers:** manipulation of air masses; full control and command of the wind.

## Appearance (detailed)

From the detailed model specs (`crimekickers_models/windman.md`,
`godot-test1/docs/characters/windman.md`):

- **Body:** humanoid, male, moderately stout, slightly loose physique — normal
  proportions, not heavily muscled but stable.
- **Head/face:** short natural-brown hair; calm expression; no beard or mustache;
  slightly rounded face with soft features.
- **Eye bandage:** completely covers both eyes. **Top portion blue, lower portion
  (above the eyebrows) red.** Fastened near the ears, fits tightly to the face.
- **Shirt:** blue T-shirt, fitted but not tight; sleeveless or short sleeves to
  mid-shoulder. **Chest emblem:** a large white / light-gray letter **"W"**, and
  below it (separated by a line) the letter **"M"** — his defining icon.
- **Shorts:** brown, knee-length, straight cut, slightly baggy.
- **Boots:** fully black, flat-soled, snug, no decoration.

### The fan

- Small handheld fan, held in the **right hand**.
- **Handle:** brown, ~25 cm, thin, wood-textured.
- **Blades:** three blades colored **green, blue, and red**, slightly transparent,
  rounded, ~10 cm each, evenly spaced around a central hub.

## Powers

- **Wind / air control (aerokinesis)** — the core canon power.
- Documented power applications across source material:
  - **Gust / blast** ("Gale Force" / "Gale Blast"): pushes enemies away, knocks
    them off balance, blows open doors, stuns on hit.
  - **Wind-lift activation:** raises wind-receptive platforms.
  - **Personal updraft / brief lift** (Hub: ~90 sec).
  - **Pressure sensing:** read atmospheric pressure at range (Hub: ~100 ft).
- **Disguise** (source material, *Shadow/Stealth Kickers*): because he looks like an
  ordinary person, he can blend in among civilians and distract enemies to avoid
  detection. *Not in the registry — treat as source material.*
