# Mr. Pierce

A non-powered supporting character from the [[Crime Kickers Hub]] continuity — a
**school authority figure** (detention administrator / principal type).

## What's known

- Appears in the case file [[C-038 — Detention Dimension]], where he **finds a portal**.
- The team actively hides from him: a Hub footer note reads *"do NOT show this to mr.
  pierce"* — framing him as the rule-enforcer / mild antagonist the heroes must
  evade.

## Open hooks

- Is he purely an obstacle, or does the portal pull him into a larger plot?
- His role only makes sense within the Hub's "school" framing — which itself is
  unreconciled with the canon origin (see [[Open Questions & Contradictions]]).
