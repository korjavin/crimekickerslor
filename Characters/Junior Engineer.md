# Junior Engineer

A recurring supporting character. A young engineer employed at **GastroDefense
Inc.** He is the only human who witnesses the AEGIS-4 fracture from start to
finish. He cares about the prototype — the others do not.

> **Role archetype:** The "person who knows" — recurring POV witness.
> Future issues may bring him back as the one human who understands what
> happened and what walked out the door.

## Canon snapshot (ratified 2026-06-14)

- **Recurring status:** confirmed.
- **Narrative function:** the only human who sees the split (Panels 2–3).
  Positioned as a recurring POV witness across future issues — the one inside
  GastroDefense who knows the truth of the fracture.
- **Relationship to the team:** does not know any of the four by name or
  identity; was present at their birth.

## Appearance (detailed, ratified 2026-06-14)

### Per-panel pose notes (S01E01)

- **Panel 1:** hugging the diving helmet to his chest; anxious protective
  expression; crouched near the helmet.
- **Panel 2:** crouched at the bench, leaning close to the headless chassis,
  mid-whisper; tender hopeful expression; back partly to the door (oblivious
  to the Lunch Lady behind him).
- **Panel 3:** diving for cover behind the rolling food-service cart, hands
  thrown up over his head; terrified wide-eyed expression; mouth open in panic.
  Small in the lower foreground for scale and comedy.
