# The Bad Guys

The generic antagonist force the Crime Kickers fight. The full title of the
[[Shadow Kickers (2D Platformer)]] game is literally **"Crime Kickers vs The Bad
Guys."** As yet they are an **undefined faction** — no name, leader, or motive.

## What exists so far (source material)

- **Shadow Kickers (2D platformer):** small red square enemies that patrol set
  routes. Killed by stomp, dash ([[Primm]]), projectile, or stunned by wind
  ([[Windman]]). A "hardcore" mode adds more, darker-red enemies. No named bosses.
- **Stealth Kickers (tactical stealth):** guards with vision cones and alert states
  (UNAWARE → SUSPICIOUS → ALERTED) defending an **enemy base**. The mission is to
  *"infiltrate enemy territory, complete objectives, and get out undetected."*

## Open hooks

- Who are "The Bad Guys"? A crime syndicate (fits "Crime Kickers")? The faction that
  fractured the original cyborg? Undefined and wide open.
- The "enemy base" of *Stealth Kickers* suggests an organized, militarized adversary.
