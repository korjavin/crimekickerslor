# Cyborg Piglet

A mechanized variant of the [[Piglet Crocodile]] from the [[Godot Endless Runner]].
**Source material**, not in the registry.

## Appearance

- A **genetically modified piglet-crocodile hybrid**: a long green crocodile snout
  on a pink piglet body.
- **Locomotion:** rolls on **four wheels instead of legs** — the "cyborg" element.

## Behavior

- Moves in straight lines, changing direction at random or when hitting obstacles.
- **Fatal on contact** with the player (intended).

## Status

Designed but **not wired into gameplay**: nothing spawns it, and its intended death
trigger (`player.game_over()`) doesn't exist on the player yet, so its collision is
currently a no-op. A documented-but-unimplemented enemy.

## Open hooks

- The "genetically modified" + "cyborg" framing hints at a **maker/antagonist** who
  builds these creatures — undefined, and a natural thread to develop. Note the
  thematic rhyme with the team's own **cyborg** origin (see [[Origin & Concept]]).
