# S01E19 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E19 — Consent Field]] (the injunction arrives; Twist 4 lands in a footnote;
the ten-day clock starts). Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and the props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is **one comic page resolved across five images** (P1–P5). It is a
**procedural episode**: nothing physical happens; the document does everything.
The visual space moves across three registers — **city morning street** (P1–P2),
**close document** (P3), **narrow daytime threshold** (P4), **rooftop evening** (P5).
No SFX appear in any panel: the heroes' four kinetic heartbeats are absent not as a
formal device but as a plain fact. No SFX boxes, no blank SFX boxes — the
absence is simply present as air.
