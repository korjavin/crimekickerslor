# S01E18 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E18 — Resonance]] (Movement IV opener — the un-make question put directly in
the room, the fragments' description given, Pho-boman's silence at maximum weight).
Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and all props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is **one comic page resolved across five images** (P1–P5). It is the
series' **most dialogue-forward episode** and its quietest visual register: no SFX, no
FIELD READ bracket, no corporate screen, no drone, no kinetic action. The space is a
**single contained nondescript interior** throughout — utilitarian, neutral, not a
stage. The four are present together in the room. The only saturated notes are the
four canonical character-color accents (blue bandage + W-shirt, mustard-gold shirt,
reddish-orange broth, violet/cyan visor) read against a warm-ambient neutral interior
palette. The resonance field between the two fragments — Windman's ghost-lines and
Primm's passive DRIFT light-bend — is visible in the air of the shared interior at
**low, constant, non-firing amplitude** throughout: not an event, not aimed, simply
running because it always runs since E15. No FIELD READ bracket. No SFX callout.
