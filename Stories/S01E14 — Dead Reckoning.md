# S01E14 — Dead Reckoning

> **Logline:** Four powers, one building, one shoulder of luck — they get him out, and they do not yet know what the room they walked through did to two of them.

> **Stage:** Broad-strokes outline. Synopsis, continuity, and threads are locked to the [[Series Arc]] (Movement II close). Panel breakdown, captions, and image prompts are a later production pass (panel-prompt-smith).

## Continuity / placement

**Movement II close.** Follows [[S01E13 — Field Notes]] (lineage; clock under 48 hours). Precedes [[S01E15 — Partial Contact]]. The rescue must beat the 72-hour transfer-to-chamber deadline from E12.

## Synopsis

The rescue. Pho-boman enters first — the only one the drone net cannot find — and navigates to sublevel 3 along the access route he read in E12. Windman and Tiebi run a pressure-and-size distraction at the loading dock to pull the Retrieval Division's attention and thin the interior drone count: Tiebi grows to maximum interior-viable size and hits his low-ceiling limit as a managed cost; Windman reads the sublevel-3 location through the building's air-distribution system and feeds Pho-boman directional guidance. Primm phases out — his field running low after 72+ hours constrained by steel, but not gone. **The four are together again.**

**But the cost is already incurred and invisible:** the reintegration chamber on sublevel 2 was in staging when Pho-boman and Primm passed through it, its preliminary alignment field running ambient. The protocol has no branch for "asset recovered by the subject's own team" — only "asset not found — escalate." It escalates, to a `[REDACTED PER COMPLIANCE PROTOCOL]` recipient not visible this issue.

## Thread advanced

- Primm is free; the team is whole. The rescue arc (E10–E14) closes on success.
- The escalation routes to an unseen recipient (paid off in E17 as AEGIS-5).

## Twist / suspense

- The chamber's ambient prep field touched both fragment signatures during the extraction. Nothing visibly happens yet. The reader holds the unease; the heroes don't know there is anything to hold. (Sets up E15's cost reveal.)
- The escalation `[REDACTED]` recipient is the new vector.

## What the audience knows that the heroes don't

- Walking through the staging chamber was not free. The series' established slapstick-competence beat (interlocking powers, like E08) here has a hidden price — the inverse of E08's "they accidentally won."
- Pho-boman, signature-less, passed through the prep field untouched; only the two fragments were contacted.

## Differentiation / what's new

The biggest ensemble action set-piece since E08 — but where E08's convergence was accidental joy, this is deliberate cooperation under a clock, and it succeeds at a cost the episode hides. The win is real and immediately undercut.
