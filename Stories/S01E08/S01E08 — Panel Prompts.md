# S01E08 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E08 — Four-Body Problem]] (the full four-way convergence — the Act III close).
Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and the props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is **one comic page resolved across six images** (P1–P6). It is the series'
**first four-handed episode**: the first time **all four heroes share a frame**, and the
first time **all four signature colors and all four SFX appear together**. The space is a
**sloped city block at night** — one block, one object (a driverless refrigerated truck),
no enclosure. The two pairs converge from **opposite ends** of the block (Windman + Tiebi
down one side-street, Primm + Pho-boman in from the other end), accidentally stop the
truck together, then face outward as four. The seam: this **closes Act III** — it receives
the Windman + Tiebi pair from [[S01E06 — Two-Body Problem]]'s two-figure close and the
Primm + Pho-boman pair from [[S01E07 — Specific Heat]]'s threshold close, and **inverts**
[[S01E01 — Some Assembly Required]] Panel 5 (four figures walking apart in four compass
directions) into four figures facing the same direction with the problem behind them.
