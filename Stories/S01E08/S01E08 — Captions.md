# S01E08 — Captions

Print-ready narration captions for [[S01E08 — Four-Body Problem]] — to letter onto the comic
page beside each picture. E08 is **caption-driven convergence comedy**: the dry case-file
narrator does most of the heavy lifting, delivering the team-feeling as a mechanical fact
rather than sentiment. Dialogue is minimal — one flat Tiebi line in Panel 5. No narration
captions in E08 ever editorialize; the comedy is entirely in the driest possible notation of
what is happening. 6 panels total; 10 narration captions plus one line of dialogue. Pairs with
[[S01E08 — Panel Prompts]].

## Captions

**Panel 1 — Four Signals, One Block**
> The truck had been unattended for eleven minutes.
>
> Four different people noticed this for four different reasons.
>
> The city did not comment.

**Panel 2 — Each Doing Their Own Math**

*(Split-register panel: four simultaneous vignettes, each with its own caption in its own corner
of the panel. No narration captions span the full panel — each belongs to its vignette.)*

> *(top-left, Windman's vignette)* The mass was moving. Something on its far side was not the mass.

> *(top-right, Primm's vignette)* The metal was approximately one freight truck. The other
> signal was approximately nothing he had a framework for.

> *(bottom-left, Tiebi's vignette)* The bridge clearance was two point four meters.

> *(bottom-right, Pho-boman's vignette)* The smell was familiar. The smell was also behind a
> wall. He found this interesting.

**Panel 3 — One-Body Problem**
> One of them tried it alone.
>
> Eight metric tons declined.
>
> The math required the others. Nobody had asked them.

**Panel 4 — Four-Body Problem**
> The truck stopped.
>
> This took four of them.
>
> None of them said anything about that.

**Panel 5 — The Air Between Them**
> The air between them had not decided what to do with itself.

*(Single narration caption; the panel also carries one line of dialogue — see lettering notes.)*

**Panel 6 — Recall**
> None of them said the word.
>
> There were four of them now.
>
> GastroDefense Inc. operated fourteen field-survey drones in the district.

## Lettering notes

- **Panel 1 caption staircase:** three lines, descending in weight. "The city did not
  comment." is the button — letter it as the quietest of the three, the dry punchline of the
  staircase.
- **Panel 2 split-register:** each vignette caption sits at its own corner of the panel, in
  the case-file box register. They do not flow into each other. The vignette for Windman
  (top-left) also contains an in-panel **FIELD READ bracket** (not a narration caption — it is
  in-panel lettering, framing Windman's perception display):
  *[FIELD READ: irregular-mass (known) / — / broth-type (warm, 62 kg)]*
  The dash placeholder in the second column (where Primm's signature would be) is the precise
  element; do not fill it.
- **Panel 3 SFX — STOMP (single):** Tiebi's lone STOMP fires as he plants and the eight-ton
  truck shrugs him off. Letter it smaller and singular — one hero's sound, not the team's. It
  is the deliberate quiet contrast to Panel 4; do not letter the P3 STOMP at P4 scale.
- **Panel 4 SFX — all four fire simultaneously:** this is the only panel in the series where
  all four SFX appear in one frame *together* (Panel 3 carries a single STOMP as the contrast
  beat). Letter them as separate callout boxes, stacked or arranged in the panel's visual
  margins:
  - *HWUF —* (Windman, front-wheel-gap compression sweep)
  - *DRIFT —* (Primm, front axle lift)
  - *STOMP —* (Tiebi, planted in the truck's rolling path)
  - *SLURP —* (Pho-boman, noodle-rope cinched on the rear axle)
- **Panel 4 caption staircase:** three lines, the most minimal in the issue. Letter them at
  equal weight, evenly spaced — the comedy is entirely in the geometry of three sentences doing
  the minimum.
- **Panel 5 dialogue:** one line of dialogue (not a narration caption), flat delivery, no
  punctuation drama:
  **TIEBI:** *You were in the freezer.*
  This is the only dialogue in the issue. Letter it as a speech bubble in the ordinary register —
  not a shout, not an aside, a flat statement. No SFX in Panel 5.
- **Panel 6 caption staircase:** three lines, descending in size and weight. "None of them
  said the word." is full weight; "There were four of them now." is slightly smaller;
  "GastroDefense Inc. operated fourteen field-survey drones in the district." is the smallest
  caption in the panel — the Act IV plant, delivered in the series' inventory register.
  The in-panel truck side-panel text (the recall notice) is legible in-art lettering, not a
  narration caption — see below.
- **Truck side-panel in-art lettering (Panel 6):** on the truck's side panel, in small
  institutional type, the corporate recall notice is rendered as in-art lettering (not a
  narration caption box):
  *UNIT RECALL PROTOCOL — RECALLED UNITS: W-01, P-02, P-03, T-04 / RETURN TO ORIGIN FACILITY.*
  Below it, the GastroDefense corporate tagline (also in-art): *Hot meals, cold justice.*
  The narrator does not point at either. The characters do not read them. They are simply in
  the frame.
- **Deliberate silence — Panel 5:** beyond the single narration caption and Tiebi's dialogue,
  Panel 5 carries no additional text. The involuntary field-overlap between Windman and Primm
  is visual only; the narrator names the air's indecision and stops. Do not fill the silence.
