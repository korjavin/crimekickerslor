# S01E12 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E12 — Thermal Gradient]] (the solo infiltration — Pho-boman walks into
GastroDefense HQ, reads the database, and walks out with a clock).
Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and the props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is **one comic page resolved across five images** (P1–P5). It is the
series' **first solo-inside-the-apparatus episode**: one hero in the enemy building,
all others waiting outside. Two visual registers never share a panel — the **outside**
(night street, fence, three figures: Windman, Tiebi, Pho-boman) and the **inside**
(warm institutional corridors, cold-storage adjacencies, a staging-area terminal).
Pho-boman carries both registers: reddish-orange warmth moving through a building of
cold fluorescent white.
