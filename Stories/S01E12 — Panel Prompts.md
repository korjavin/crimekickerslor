# S01E12 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E12 — Thermal Gradient]] (the solo infiltration — Pho-boman walks into
GastroDefense HQ and comes back with a clock).
Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and the props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is a **5-panel comic page** in two visual registers: the **outside**
(three figures at a street-level remove from GastroDefense HQ, night, waiting) and
the **inside** (warm institutional corridors, cold-storage adjacencies, a
staging-area terminal). Pho-boman carries both registers — reddish-orange warmth
inside a building that is all cold fluorescent. **Primm is absent throughout** —
held offscreen on sublevel 3, referenced only as structure and database entry.
