# S01E14 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E14 — Dead Reckoning]] (the Movement II close — the four-way rescue of Primm
from GastroDefense HQ, with a hidden cost the heroes do not see).
Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and all props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is **one comic page resolved across six images** (P1–P6). The spatial
grammar is **two registers in parallel**: the loading-dock exterior (Tiebi + Windman)
and the GastroDefense HQ interior (Pho-boman + Primm), with Windman's air-handling
field-read as the connective tissue. The episode's tone moves from dry/wide (P1) to
kinetic/exterior (P2) to quiet/institutional-interior (P3) to efficient/kinetic (P4)
to coldly procedural (P5) to warm-with-cold-underneath (P6). The rescue succeeds; the
cost is invisible to the heroes and present to the reader.
