# S01E15 — Partial Contact

> **Logline:** Two days after the rescue, the two halves of one body realize the thing between them never went back to sleep.

> **Stage:** Broad-strokes outline. Synopsis, continuity, and threads are locked to the [[Series Arc]] (Movement III opener). Panel breakdown, captions, and image prompts are a later production pass (panel-prompt-smith).

## Continuity / placement

**Movement III opener.** Follows [[S01E14 — Dead Reckoning]] (rescue succeeded; chamber prep field touched both fragments). Precedes [[S01E16 — Prior Art]]. The un-make question moves from document (E09) to lived sensation here.

## Synopsis

Two days after the rescue, Windman and Primm notice the fragment-resonance has changed. The involuntary flutter the audience first saw in [[S01E08 — Four-Body Problem]] (the residual field-entanglement "in the air between them") is now an **ongoing signal** — not controllable, but constant: a low-frequency presence both fragments feel at all times, not only in close proximity.

The cause is E14's extraction: both passed through the reintegration chamber's ambient prep field. No reintegration occurred, but the preliminary alignment sequence contacted both AEGIS-4 fragment signatures at low amplitude and woke the resonance from dormant to active. Neither has vocabulary for what they now carry. What they know: the un-make question is no longer theoretical. They can feel what "one body" means in a way they could not before — the question of whether reintegration would un-make them is now a question about a sensation, not a clause.

## Thread advanced

- The rescue's cost is named: the resonance is permanently active.
- The un-make dilemma becomes a daily lived reality for the two fragments (drives Movement IV).

## Twist / suspense

- The reader, who saw the prep field in E14, understands the cause before the heroes diagnose it.

## What the audience knows that the heroes don't

- The resonance is the two true fragments being partially re-entangled — exactly the state the recall wants to complete. They walked out of the building carrying a piece of the un-making with them.
- Tiebi's lesser incident-signature echo becomes slightly more legible after the shift; Pho-boman feels nothing — and, for the first time, is *aware* of his absence from the field rather than simply carrying it.

## Differentiation / what's new

A quiet, character-first episode after the action of E14 — the cost beat. It rhymes with the diaspora arc's lonely-funny register, but the loneliness is now shared and structural: two beings discovering they are tied together by the very thing that could erase them both.
