# S01E15 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E15 — Partial Contact]] (Movement III opener — two days after the rescue,
the resonance between the two true fragments wakes from dormant to constant).
Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and the props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is **one comic page resolved across five images** (P1–P5). It is a **cost
episode** — the first in the series whose entire subject is something invisible, felt
rather than shown. No action, no timer, no corporate antagonist on-page. The visual
space opens on **day-lit street** (the city two days later), moves into a **nondescript
interior**, and closes on a **split-frame**: Windman and Primm each at their own
threshold, the resonance running between them across the gap of the city. No SFX
callout appears in the closing panel — the resonance is a state, not an event.
