# S01E16 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E16 — Prior Art]] (the archive episode — the double-decode of the AEGIS-3 prior
reintegration log; the CATERING-004A collapse; the "still-warm" signal in the walls).
Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and the props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is **one comic page resolved across five images** (P1–P5). It is the
series' **coldest palette to date**: no warm color present until Pho-boman enters, and
his reddish-orange broth is the only source of warmth in every interior frame. The
space is a **disused former GastroDefense processing facility** — industrial dormancy,
not dramatic ruins. Captions are dry case-file register throughout. Zero to minimal
dialogue; the AEGIS-3 prior-reintegration log does all the talking.
