# S01E16 — Prior Art

> **Logline:** They follow an old signal to an empty building and read a log that tells them two things: this has been done before, and the soup that made one of them was shipped to un-make all of them.

> **Stage:** Broad-strokes outline. Synopsis, continuity, and threads are locked to the [[Series Arc]] (Movement III). Panel breakdown, captions, and image prompts are a later production pass (panel-prompt-smith). **Double-decode episode** (Twists 3 and 6 land together).

## Continuity / placement

**Movement III.** Follows [[S01E15 — Partial Contact]] (resonance active). Precedes [[S01E17 — Cascade Failure]]. Pursues the "older signal" the Junior Engineer logged in [[S01E13 — Field Notes]]. Pays off the cargo manifest planted in [[S01E08 — Four-Body Problem]].

## Synopsis

Following the notebook's old signal, the four reach a disused former GastroDefense processing facility where a "still-warm" AEGIS-3 fragment signal lingers in the structure (Tiebi's old credentials still open the exterior panel). The prior reintegration log decodes two things at once.

**Twist 3 — disposal has been executed before.** AEGIS-3's reintegration was completed; one of its non-specification outputs was disposed of under GD-Standard-7 — the same standard that covers Pho-boman. The log uses only the standard's language and **does not describe what the disposed entity was** (author-ratified: left unspecified — the form, not the being). Disposal is not a threat. It is a filed, signed-off historical fact with a case number and a date.

**Twist 6 — the truck cargo was the reagent.** The same log references CATERING-004A and a frozen broth reagent shipped from GastroDefense Catering Division. The cargo from the E08 truck — *FROZEN PHO BROTH (BULK), DESTINATION: RECALL PROCESSING CENTER* — was the reagent for **their** reintegration. The E08 team-joy beat was the closest any of them came to being un-made, and Pho-boman's origin-broth and the un-making reagent are the same lineage.

## Thread advanced

- Prior iterations fully real; disposal established as precedent, not threat.
- The broth retro-charge closes: Pho-boman's origin and the reintegration reagent connect in the heroes' knowledge.

## Twist / suspense

- **Twist 3** and **Twist 6** land in the same issue — the episode's combined weight is its own beat.
- The "still-warm" AEGIS-3 signal is identified but **not pursued** — a beyond-20 seed. The Junior Engineer's notebook note beside it reads: *don't know what this means yet.* Neither do they.

## What the audience knows that the heroes don't

- The disposed AEGIS-3 secondary output is the historical precedent for Pho-boman and Tiebi's filed fates. The asymmetry the audience has tracked since E09 is now shown to have already been executed on someone.
- "Still warm" means reintegration into a prototype does not fully consolidate a signal — a quiet implication about what un-making actually does, reserved.

## Differentiation / what's new

The series' coldest reveal, delivered as archival paperwork in an empty building. No antagonist present — just a log. It recontextualizes the whole season: the four are a re-run of a procedure GastroDefense has completed before, reagent and all.

## Reserved / open threads

- The "still-warm" AEGIS-3 fragment signal: beyond-20 thread, never approached on-page.
