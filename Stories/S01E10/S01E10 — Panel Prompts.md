# S01E10 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E10 — Half-Life]] (the midpoint capture — Primm taken, three free, the gap
in the air). Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and the props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is **one comic page resolved across five images** (P1–P5). It is the series'
**midpoint** and the **first time the team loses**: the capture of Primm by a GastroDefense
Retrieval Division field team executing standard equipment-recovery procedure. The capture
is incidental — the standard steel-frame containment unit happens to work. Nobody designed
this trap. The space traverses two locations: an **outer city district** (pre-dawn streets,
low industrial facades, the Retrieval Division van on approach) and a **Retrieval Division
containment staging area** (a fenced chain-link lot with a flatbed, one floodlight,
industrial-standard portable steel-frame holding structure). The episode closes on three
figures outside and one inside — the first time the four-figure closing grammar breaks.
