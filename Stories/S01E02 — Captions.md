## Lettering notes

- In this issue the **narration captions ARE the primary lettering** (E02 is
  near-wordless), set in dry case-file caption boxes. They are the same lines locked
  in-panel in [[S01E02 — Panel Prompts]] — this sheet just consolidates them for the
  letterer.
- **Non-caption in-panel lettering** (locked in [[S01E02 — Panel Prompts]], not part of
  the narration track):
  - Panel 2 — SFX *"HWUF—"*, small, integrated into the gust-trace.
  - Panel 4 — the cut-off offscreen voice *"Hey, is that—"*, very small lettering; the
    **speaker is never shown** and the line is never finished (the city stays
    indifferent; the issue stays sealed).
  - Panel 5 beat A — *"Windman."* is the **smallest, most offhand caption in the issue**,
    bottom-right. It is the title payoff; let it whisper, not announce.
- **Deliberate silences — do not fill them.** Panels 2, 4, and 5-beat-B carry no
  narration caption on purpose. Lettering every panel would flatten the loneliness the
  issue is built on ("big silence"). The art + the three non-caption lines above are the
  whole text of the page.
- **Open thread (from the script):** whether the final caption reads bare *"Windman."*
  or em-dash *"— Windman."* (signalling internal arrival rather than narration) is
  unresolved — letter it bare per the current draft; flag for the author. See
  [[S01E02 — Feel the Room]] Open threads.
- **Voice continuity:** the dry, numeric-deadpan turn in Panel 1 ("approximately four
  minutes") is the same register as S01E01's captions ("eleven months… eleven minutes"),
  kept consistent so the two issues read as one show.
