# S01E19 — Consent Field

> **Logline:** The only one of them who legally exists is handed a court order, and the case number on it is older than all four of them.

> **Stage:** Broad-strokes outline. Synopsis, continuity, and threads are locked to the [[Series Arc]] (Movement IV). Panel breakdown, captions, and image prompts are a later production pass (panel-prompt-smith).

## Continuity / placement

**Movement IV.** Follows [[S01E18 — Resonance]] (the question put directly). Precedes [[S01E20 — Return to Origin Facility]]. Starts the ten-day clock that runs into the finale-turn.

## Synopsis

The law-enforcement client exercises its "alternative enforcement" option from E17: a legal **injunction**, filed in a city court and served on **Tiebi** — the only Crime Kicker with a legal identity, the only one who can be served a court document. It requires the four to present at GastroDefense's Origin Facility within **ten days** for compliance with the original recall specification: reintegration for Windman and Primm, disposal for Pho-boman and Tiebi.

The injunction's case header carries a number that shares a parent filing with the AEGIS-3 prior recall the heroes read in [[S01E16 — Prior Art]] (cross-referenced via the Junior Engineer's notebook). **The four were named in an active legal action before they had names** (Twist 4): *"Project AEGIS-4 — Status: Recalled"* in [[S01E01 — Some Assembly Required]] was a legal status, not just a corporate record. The client has been filing since before the fracture. The injunction does not ask whether they consent — the current spec (Rev. 7) has no such field, and Appendix C was never written. Tiebi reads it once, shows nothing of what he thinks, and puts it in his pocket.

## Thread advanced

- The legal clock begins; the un-make question is at maximum pressure.
- The case-number match ties the four to the prior iterations and to a pre-fracture legal action.

## Twist / suspense

- **Twist 4 — the injunction is a revival.** The four are not a new problem; they are a re-filing of an old one.
- Ten days. No decision yet. The drone net thickens after the filing; the Junior Engineer goes quiet. (Pressure tightens toward E20.)

## What the audience knows that the heroes don't

- "Status: Recalled" was always a legal status — the E01 closer recontextualized as a docket entry.
- The client and its enforcement mechanism beyond this injunction remain offscreen — a beyond-20 engine.

## Differentiation / what's new

The threat finally takes an active form — and it is a court order, served on a man because he is the only one the law recognizes as a person. The most menacing beat of the season is paperwork with a deadline, handed over without a word.
