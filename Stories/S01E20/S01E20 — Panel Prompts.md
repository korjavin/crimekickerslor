# S01E20 — Panel Prompts

Image-generation prompts and episode-specific visual canon for
[[S01E20 — Return to Origin Facility]] (the season turn — the third option, the cost,
the Lunch Lady's second word, *[FIELD PROCESSING]*).
Built by the **panel-prompt-smith** agent.

> Each prompt is **self-contained** — image models have no memory, so every prompt
> restates the art style, every character in the frame, and the props in full. The
> global art style and recurring descriptors live in [[Visual Style Guide]]; reuse the
> wordings below **verbatim** across panels for consistency.

This issue is **one comic page resolved across six images** (P1–P6). It is the **season
turn** — the Act IV close and the series' first open turn: not a resolution, a deferral
with cost. The space is the **GastroDefense Origin Facility** — the reintegration annex
lobby (sublevel 2 below), the cafeteria staircase half-landing, and the cafeteria floor
(sublevel 1), followed by an exterior street close. The facility is operational and
indifferent: white tile, institutional green trim, fluorescent overhead, posted safety
notices, a steam table. The four warm hero-colors gathered in one cold institutional space
is the visual tension the arc has built toward.
