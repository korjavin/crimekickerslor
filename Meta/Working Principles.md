# Working Principles

Rules that apply to all agents and all work in this vault.

## Language

**All vault content is written in English.** This applies regardless of what
language the author uses when dictating or communicating. Conversations with the
author may happen in any language; files written to the vault — notes, character
pages, story stubs, edits — are always in English.

*Reason: the vault is a single knowledge base. Mixed languages break
searchability, consistency, and readability for any future collaborator or agent.*
